WHAT'S KB727

KB727 is a global certification program that advocates for social responsibility in tutoring.

WHY KB727

Money shouldn't dictate access to education. We want to live in a world where everyone have access to a great education — not just those whose families can afford one. We encourage and support more low-income families and those with financial difficulties to access free tutorials. As of October 2024, we have booked more than 200 interviews in Shanghai, Seattle, and Buffalo, and have awarded 15 certifications.

HOW WE WORK

The same tutor who provides free tutoring for low-income families and the financially disadvantaged for more than 6 months will pass KB727 certification and receive rewards, as well as commercial promotion support. Authentication documents and delivery of reward items will be confirmed by the official email. We're the oldest organization doing this work. We've been doing this for a long time and we've learned a lot along the way.

WANT TO GET INVOLVED?

We are willing to cooperate with more tutors, media, institutions, and donors to jointly provide support for socially responsible tutorials, please contact us through the official email.
